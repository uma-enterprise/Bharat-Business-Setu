About Company Name Change

A company can change its name at any time by passing a special resolution and obtaining approval from the Registrar of Companies. The new name must comply with naming guidelines and should not be similar to existing companies or trademarks.

Process

1

Name Availability

Check and reserve new name with RUN (Reserve Unique Name) service.

2

Board Meeting

Pass board resolution to convene EGM for name change.

3

Special Resolution

Pass special resolution at EGM with 75% majority.

4

File INC-24

File Form INC-24 with ROC along with all documents.

5

New Certificate

Receive fresh Certificate of Incorporation with new name.
