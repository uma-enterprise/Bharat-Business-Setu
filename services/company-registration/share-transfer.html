About Share Transfer

Share transfer involves transferring ownership of shares from one person to another. This can be a sale, gift, or transmission (in case of death). Proper documentation and stamp duty are mandatory.

Documents Required

From Transferor

  • Share Certificates
  • Signed Share Transfer Deed (SH-4)
  • PAN Card

From Transferee

  • PAN Card
  • Address Proof
  • Signed Transfer Deed
