Ways to Remove a Director

Resignation (DIR-11)

Director voluntarily resigns by submitting resignation letter.

Shareholder Resolution

Removal by ordinary resolution at general meeting with special notice.

Tribunal Order

NCLT can order removal in case of fraud or mismanagement.

Automatic Vacation

Automatic vacation of office due to disqualification under Section 164.
