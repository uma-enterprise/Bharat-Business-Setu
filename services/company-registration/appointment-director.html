About Director Appointment

A company can appoint additional directors to the Board as per provisions of the Companies Act, 2013. The new director must obtain DIN (Director Identification Number) and DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) before appointment.

Types of Directors

Executive Director

Involved in day-to-day operations and management.

Independent Director

Required for listed companies, brings outside perspective.

Nominee Director

Appointed by investors or financial institutions.

Additional Director

Appointed between AGMs, holds office until next AGM.

Documents Required

From New Director

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Address Proof
  • DSC (we can obtain)

From Company

  • Board Resolution
  • Consent Letter (DIR-2)
  • Declaration (DIR-8)
  • CIN Number
