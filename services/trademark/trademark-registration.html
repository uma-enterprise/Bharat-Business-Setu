What is Trademark Registration?

A Trademark is a unique symbol, word, phrase, logo, design, or combination that identifies and distinguishes the source of goods or services of one party from those of others. Trademark Registration provides you with the exclusive legal right to use your mark and prevents others from using similar marks.

Important: Once registered, a trademark is valid for 10 years and can be renewed indefinitely, providing lifetime protection for your brand.

Benefits of Trademark Registration

Legal Protection

Exclusive right to use the mark across India with legal remedies against infringement.

Use ® Symbol

Right to use the ® symbol which adds credibility and deters copycats.

Business Asset

Trademark becomes an intangible asset that can be sold, franchised, or licensed.

Global Protection

Basis for filing trademark applications in foreign countries.

Prevent Misuse

Legal action against anyone using similar marks for similar goods/services.

Brand Value

Enhances brand credibility and trust among customers and investors.

Registration Process

1

Trademark Search

We conduct comprehensive search to ensure your trademark is unique and registrable. Takes 1-2 days.

2

Application Filing

Submit trademark application (Form TM-A) with all required documents. Takes 1 day.

3

Vienna Codification

Trademark Registry assigns codes and allots examination date. Takes 1-2 months.

4

Examination

Examiner reviews application; may raise objections which we handle. Takes 6-12 months.

5

Publication in Journal

Accepted trademark is published for public opposition (4 months window).

6

Registration Certificate

If no opposition, trademark is registered and certificate issued. Takes 1-2 months.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take?

The complete trademark registration process typically takes 12-18 months. However, you get TM™ status immediately after filing, which provides protection during the registration process.

What is the difference between ™ and ®?

™ (trademark symbol) can be used immediately after filing an application. ® (registered trademark symbol) can only be used after the trademark is officially registered by the Registry.

Can I register a trademark in multiple classes?

Yes, you can file a multi-class application or separate applications for each class. Each class requires separate government fees. There are 45 classes in total - 34 for goods and 11 for services.

What happens if my trademark gets objected?

If the examiner raises objections, you need to file a response within 30 days. Our Standard and Premium packages include objection handling. Common objections are related to similarity with existing marks or lack of distinctiveness.

Is trademark registration valid throughout India?

Yes, once registered, your trademark is valid and protected throughout India. For international protection, you need to file in respective countries or use the Madrid Protocol for multiple country filings.
