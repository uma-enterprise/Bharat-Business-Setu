What is FSSAI Modification?

FSSAI Modification is the process of updating or changing the details in your existing FSSAI license/registration. This includes adding new food products, changing business address, updating proprietor/partner details, or making other amendments to your food license.

Note: Any change in your food business details must be updated in FSSAI within 30 days to maintain compliance.

Types of Modifications

Add Food Products

Add new food categories or products to your existing license.

Address Change

Update business premises address in your FSSAI license.

Proprietor Change

Update owner/proprietor details or add/remove partners.

Business Name Change

Update your food business or company name.

Capacity Change

Update production or storage capacity details.

License Upgrade

Upgrade from Basic to State or State to Central license.

Documents Required

Basic Documents

  • Existing FSSAI License copy
  • Updated identity proof
  • Passport size photograph

For Changes

  • New address proof (for address change)
  • Updated food product list
  • NOC for capacity increase
  • Board resolution (for companies)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does FSSAI modification take?

FSSAI modification typically takes 7-15 working days depending on the type of modification and department workload.

Does modification change my FSSAI number?

No, your 14-digit FSSAI license number remains the same. Only the details mentioned in the license are updated.

Is there a government fee for modification?

Minor modifications may not have government fees. However, for license upgrades or major changes, additional government fees may apply.
