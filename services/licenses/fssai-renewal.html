What is FSSAI Renewal?

FSSAI Renewal is the process of extending the validity of your existing FSSAI license/registration before it expires. Every FSSAI license has a validity period of 1-5 years, after which it must be renewed to continue operating your food business legally.

Important: Apply for renewal at least 30 days before expiry. Late renewal attracts a penalty of ₹100 per day of delay.

When Should You Renew?

30 Days Before Expiry

Ideal time to apply for renewal to ensure seamless continuity without penalties.

Check License Status

Regularly check your FSSAI portal for license validity and renewal alerts.

Multi-Year Renewal

Renew for 1-5 years at once to save on annual government fees and effort.

Documents Required for Renewal

Basic Documents

  • Existing FSSAI License copy
  • Identity proof of applicant
  • Passport size photograph

Business Documents

  • Updated list of food products
  • Proof of premises (if changed)
  • Business continuity proof

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I don't renew my FSSAI license on time?

If you fail to renew before expiry, a late fee of ₹100 per day is charged. If the license expires, you cannot legally operate your food business until renewed.

Can I change my license type during renewal?

Yes, if your business turnover has changed, you may need to upgrade from Basic to State or State to Central license during renewal.

Is the FSSAI license number same after renewal?

Yes, your 14-digit FSSAI license number remains the same after renewal. Only the validity dates change.
