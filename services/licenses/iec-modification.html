What is IEC Modification?

IEC Modification is the process of updating or amending the details in your existing Import Export Code. This includes changes to business address, adding/removing directors, updating bank account details, or correcting any errors in the IEC certificate.

Annual Update Mandatory: As per DGFT guidelines, IEC holders must update their IEC details on DGFT portal annually (typically April-June) even if there are no changes.

Types of IEC Modifications

Address Change

Update registered business address in your IEC.

Bank Details Update

Change or add bank account linked to IEC.

Add/Remove Directors

Update director/partner information.

Contact Details

Update email, phone number, or mobile.

Entity Name Change

Update business name as per ROC records.

Annual Update

Mandatory yearly update on DGFT portal.
