What is FSSAI Annual Return?

FSSAI Annual Return is a mandatory compliance requirement for all State and Central FSSAI license holders. It requires food business operators to submit an annual return containing details about their food production, sales, and other business activities during the financial year.

Due Date: FSSAI Annual Return must be filed by 31st May every year for the previous financial year (April to March).

Who Must File FSSAI Annual Return?

State License Holders

All food businesses with State FSSAI license must file annual return.

Central License Holders

All food businesses with Central FSSAI license must file annual return.

Basic Registration

Basic FSSAI registration holders are exempt from annual return filing.

Information Required

Production Details

  • Quantity of food manufactured/processed
  • Category-wise production data
  • Raw material sourced

Financial Details

  • Annual turnover from food business
  • Sales volume details
  • Import/Export quantity (if applicable)
